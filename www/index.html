Prediction & parameters

Loss coefficient calibration (cooldown test)

Measures how fast the pool cools with the heat pump off and no sun, then back-solves the loss coefficient. Requires background polling. Auto-aborts if the heat pump turns on or solar is detected.

Run now

Starts immediately and runs for the duration you set. Make sure the heat pump is off first.

Schedule for tonight

Waits until the window starts, then measures until the window ends. Duration above is not used — the end time stops the test.

Heat pump COP (datasheet baseline)

Add the COP values from your heat pump manual. Each row is one air temperature point; values are interpolated by outdoor air temperature.
